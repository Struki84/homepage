GENERAL INFORMATION

I'm a software developer, engineer, musician, martial artist from Zagreb. At the moment I'm reevaluating my goals and taking some new directions in life. As a result I founded a technology brokering guild called Secret Project, with a co founder and a good friend Tomislav Kozačinski.

Over the years I've included myself in various projects. In addition to my degree in Mechanical Engineering, I'm a recored musician and been dedicated martial artist for almost 10 years. I'm in constant search for new things to do, learn and experience.

After 10 years in software development, and a degree specialized in CAD product development, I turned my focus more on developing new products by closing multiple science and engineering disciplines. This idea has taken form in small company founded by Tomislav Kozačinski and myself, called Sintezis.
